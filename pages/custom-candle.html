Custom Candle

Description

  • Custom Candle Co

I developed the website for Custom Candle to showcase its commitment to creating eco-friendly, non-toxic, and long-lasting candles. Built using WordPress and Elementor, the website highlights the brand's story, focusing on its mission to provide safe and sustainable candles for people with sensitivities, such as allergies and asthma.

The site features a clean, modern design with easy navigation, optimized content, and a user-friendly interface that educates visitors about the importance of non-toxic ingredients. Additionally, I incorporated e-commerce functionality to allow customers to easily explore and purchase custom-made candles, aligning with the brand's vision of providing high-quality, safe, and aromatic products.
