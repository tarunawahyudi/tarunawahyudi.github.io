I designed an informative and user-friendly website for CGS GripStrip Products Ltd., showcasing their innovative GripStrip® Anti-Slip Stair Treads. Established in 2007, CGS GripStrip specializes in manufacturing high-quality anti-slip products designed and built in North America, with shipping available across Canada and the USA.

The website highlights the key benefits of GripStrip’s stair treads, including enhanced safety to prevent slips and falls on various surfaces such as stairs, ramps, and decks. The treads are noted for their durability and resistance to harsh weather conditions, pressure washing, shoveling, and salting. Each tread is pre-punched and slotted for easy installation, eliminating the need for additional drilling and ensuring a secure fit.

Additionally, the site features GripStrip’s new PVC-based anti-slip tape introduced in 2020, offering a practical solution for situations where screw-down products are not feasible. In 2023, they also launched a Barefoot/Pet friendly version of their popular treads, providing excellent traction with a gentler surface for feet and pets. The website effectively communicates the company's commitment to safety and innovation, making it easy for customers to find and purchase the right products for their needs.
