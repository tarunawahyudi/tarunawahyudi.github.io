Secondbrain

Description

  • www.secondbrain.dev

  • 5 Nov, 2024

SecondBrain is a simple and intuitive web app designed to boost productivity, similar to Notion. Built with Next.js and Convex, it helps users easily document and organize their work. Whether for personal tasks or team collaboration, SecondBrain makes it effortless to capture ideas, manage projects, and stay organized, making it the perfect tool for everyone looking to improve their workflow.
