Takapaukura

Description

  • Takapaukura

I created the website for Takapaukura, a Māori education consultancy, to reflect its mission of promoting lifelong learning in Māori language and culture. The site is designed to showcase their range of services, from consultation on language incorporation and organizational change to professional learning solutions and resource development.

With a clean and engaging layout, the website highlights their customized programs and workshops aimed at expanding cultural knowledge and understanding. Through testimonials, visitors can see the positive impact of Takapaukura’s passionate and effective teaching approach. The website aligns with Takapaukura’s vision to normalize the use of the Māori language, fostering cultural understanding and capability in diverse contexts.
