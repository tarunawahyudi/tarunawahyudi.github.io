Description

  • Waikato Aviation

The website for Waikato Aviation invites visitors to explore a range of aviation services, from the Diploma in Aviation program for aspiring commercial pilots to exhilarating flight experiences and customizable charter flights. It also promotes their Aviators Club, where enthusiasts can join organized trips and events.

With a celebration of their 90th anniversary on the horizon, the site keeps visitors informed about upcoming events, such as club competitions. For those interested in aviation adventures or career opportunities, the website provides comprehensive information, along with easy booking options. Stay connected through their Instagram for the latest news and updates, or contact them directly for inquiries.
