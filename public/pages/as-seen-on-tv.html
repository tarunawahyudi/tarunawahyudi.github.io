As Seen On TV

Description

  • As Seen On TV

I developed this website to highlight the expertise and services of a marketing company led by Kevin Harrington, a pioneer in direct and brand response television. Built using WordPress and Elementor, the site features information about the company, its services, expertise, successful campaigns, and celebrity influencer videos, showcasing a history of over $5 billion in global sales.

The website provides a comprehensive overview of their offerings, including video production, media buying, digital services, and consulting, with a focus on omnichannel marketing strategies to help brands grow. It also includes resources like FAQs, blog posts, and opportunities for product submissions and future collaborations, reflecting the company's commitment to driving brand success through innovative marketing campaigns.
